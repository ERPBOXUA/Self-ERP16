Self ERP - Ukrainian company integrator of complex automated solutions for business development and management based on Odoo 16 Enterprise

FEA for Ukraine

The module adds additional functionality to the system that allows:

  • to form the cost of imported goods (taking into account the advances made to a foreign supplier)
  • to generate income from export (taking into account advances received from foreign buyers)
  • to display transactions for the purchase and sale of currency
